Doro Wot

Doro Wat -Ethiopian Chicken Stew -slowly simmered in a blend of robust spices. Easy thick, comforting, delicious, and so easy to make!
  1. Cuisine African
  2. Prep Time 20 minutes
  3. Cook Time 1 hour
  4. Total Time 1 hour 20 minutes
  5. Servings 6
  6. Calories 471 kcal
  7. Author Immaculate Bites
  8. Ingredients
  9. 3 Tablespoons Spiced butter Sub with Cooking oil or more
  10. 2-3 medium onions sliced
  11. 1/4 cup canola oil
  12. 2 Tablespoons Berbere Spice
  13. 1 Tablespoon minced garlic
  14. ½ Tablespoon minced ginger
  15. 3- 3½- pound whole chicken cut in pieces or chicken thighs
  16. 1 Tablespoon tomato paste
  17. ½ Tablespoon paprika
  18. 1 Tablespoon dried basil optional
  19. 4-6 Large soft boiled egg shelled removed
  20. 1-2 Lemons Freshly Squeezed (adjust to taste)
  21. Salt and pepper to taste
